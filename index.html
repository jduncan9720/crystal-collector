Crystal Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random target number at the start of each game.

Clicking on one of the crytsals below will start the game and add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by clicking the crystals until your score matches the target number.

The value of each crystal will vary from game to game.

When you win or lose, click another gem to match the next target number and play again.

Good Luck!!
